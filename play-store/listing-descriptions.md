## Full description (4000 chars max)

PrivAI is your private workspace for notes, voice transcripts, and document scans — built for people who want powerful AI tools without sending data to the cloud.

**Absolute on-device privacy**
Every core feature runs locally on your phone. Notes, speech-to-text, OCR, and summaries are processed on your device. No account required. No cloud uploads for your content.

**Workspace notes**
Capture ideas, meeting notes, and research in a clean, organized vault. Tag entries, search instantly, and open full detail views when you need more context. Your workspace stays on your phone.

**Voice transcription**
Record and transcribe speech in real time. Turn conversations, lectures, and voice memos into searchable text — handled on-device, ready to review or summarize.

**Document OCR**
Scan photos and images to extract text offline. Choose fast mode for quick reads or high-accuracy mode for dense documents. Copy results securely or save them alongside your notes.

**On-device AI summaries**
Get bullet-point summaries, keyword highlights, and sentiment hints from your notes and transcripts — powered by lightweight local NLP, not a remote server. Tap summarize on any note and see results in seconds, even without internet.

**One app, three tabs**
Switch between Workspace Notes, Audio Transcripts, and OCR Extracts from a single dashboard. See counts at a glance and jump straight to what matters.

**Built for privacy-first workflows**
Whether you are documenting security reviews, drafting offline, or keeping sensitive work off shared services, PrivAI keeps processing on your hardware.

**Why PrivAI?**
• Notes, transcription, and OCR in one place
• 100% offline processing for core features
• Fast search across your local library
• On-device summaries — no API keys needed for basic intelligence
• Dark, focused UI designed for long reading sessions

Your files. Your phone. Your rules.

Download PrivAI and take back control of your personal knowledge workspace.
